About House of Gift Cards

Our mission is to provide a platform for users, from individuals to large corporate donors, to purchase gift cards for those in need. The vision of this project is to provide a means for donors to gift a wide range of commodities from food, to utilities, to clothing.

House of Gift Cards is a streamlined app that will cut down the time and energy individually spent on giving, and by doing so, will fulfill more requests and realise the maximum benefit of these claims. This centralized platform will allow easy collection of the data, allow visualization of individual impact, and the total impact we have made as a community. Additionally, this system will connect the requester with the donor, to establish a deeper, long-lasting connection beyond just a meaningful contribution.
